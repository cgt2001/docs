## What is Lightspark?

Lightspark provides enterprise-grade infrastructure for the Lightning Network, enabling instant, low-cost Bitcoin transactions at scale. Our platform abstracts away the complexity of running Lightning nodes, managing channels, and handling liquidity.

## Why Lightspark?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Instant Payments">
    Lightning Network transactions settle in milliseconds, not minutes. Perfect for real-time payments, micropayments, and high-frequency use cases.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Low Fees">
    Transaction fees on Lightning are a fraction of on-chain costs, making small payments economically viable.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Enterprise-Ready">
    Our infrastructure handles the complexity of channel management, liquidity, and routing so you can focus on building your product.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Compliance Built-In">
    Meet regulatory requirements with our built-in compliance tools and reporting.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
